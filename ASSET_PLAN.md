# Asset Replacement Plan

This prototype uses only simple shapes, gradients, and UI text. Nothing here should be treated as final production art or audio.

## Replace Later

- Player aircraft silhouette and animation frames
- Enemy ship variants and boss concepts
- Projectile sprites and impact effects
- Background environment art for different stages
- Title logo and polished UI panels
- Sound effects for shots, hits, explosions, and UI actions
- Background music with original composition

## Suggested Style Direction

- Clean arcade sci-fi look
- High readability on small portrait screens
- Strong contrast between player, enemies, bullets, and background
- Distinct silhouettes for each enemy tier
- Lightweight effects suitable for browser and mobile packaging

## Packaging-Friendly Guidance

- Keep final assets in predictable folders such as `assets/images` and `assets/audio`
- Prefer sprite sheets or compact image atlases for mobile performance
- Export audio in web-safe formats such as `.ogg` and `.mp3`
- Maintain original-source files separately from runtime exports
